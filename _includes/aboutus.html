The mission and the team behind aquilax

Our mission

Our mission is to have a positive impact on climate change by moving billions of pounds of investments into clean energy and truly green, sustainable companies. Our platform will provide a simple way to invest into a targeted portfolio of publicly listed companies that are already making an impact in tackling climate change. Sectors covered by aquilax include clean energy, clean technology, sustainable food, smart mobility and recycling. We have raised £2 million in funding to build and launch the app, and our investors include a venture capital fund backed by the British Business Bank.
